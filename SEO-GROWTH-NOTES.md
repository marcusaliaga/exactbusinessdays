# Sustainable SEO growth notes

## What this release adds

- A useful 2026–2027 holiday and business-day dataset in four CSV files.
- A citation-friendly data page with government source links and Dataset structured data.
- A free responsive embed tool with ordinary visible attribution.
- Focused country pages for Germany, Japan, France, Ireland, New Zealand, Singapore, and the Netherlands.
- All U.S. states plus Washington, DC and all German states inside the calculator, without generating thin state pages.
- A business-days-from-today page, a monthly comparison table, and a business-days-vs-calendar-days explainer.

## Safe backlink approach

The strongest link targets are resources where the tool or dataset genuinely helps the reader: payroll and HR resource libraries, project-management guides, shipping and logistics references, legal-administration explainers, spreadsheet-template roundups, public-data catalogs, and developer resource lists.

Do not buy links, trade links at scale, mass-submit to low-quality directories, publish spun guest posts, or force hidden/keyword-stuffed attribution. Those tactics can create spam-policy risk.

## Measurement

Track new links in Search Console, referral visits in Analytics, downloads of `/data/*.csv`, visits to `/embed/`, and search impressions by country page. Evaluate quarterly; avoid judging the project only by week-to-week clicks.
